# Mojuru is Almost Here

Mojuru is a <span style="color: var(--color-primary)">free</span>, fast, customizable streaming app for iOS where you **bring your own content**.  
Built for <span style="color: var(--color-primary)">smooth playback</span>, <span style="color: var(--color-primary)">plugin support</span>, and <span style="color: var(--color-primary)">total personalization</span>.

## Features

- **Watch Tracking:** Sync your history and progress with <span style="color: var(--color-primary)">AniList</span>
- **Plugins:** Bring your <span style="color: var(--color-primary)">own content</span>
- **Customization:** Control <span style="color: var(--color-primary)">layout</span>, <span style="color: var(--color-primary)">behavior</span>, and <span style="color: var(--color-primary)">settings</span>
- **Subtitle Support:** Soft and hard subtitles with <span style="color: var(--color-primary)">language</span> and <span style="color: var(--color-primary)">style options</span>
- **Auto Skip:** Automatically skip <span style="color: var(--color-primary)">intros and outros</span>
- **Smooth UI:** Inspired by <span style="color: var(--color-primary)">iOS design</span> and performance
