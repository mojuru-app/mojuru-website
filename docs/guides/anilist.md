# AniList Guide

This guide provides information on how to use your **AniList account** with Mojuru.

## Logging in with AniList

To authenticate your AniList account:

1. Open **Settings** in the Mojuru app.  
2. Tap **Login with AniList**.  
3. Wait for the app to restart. Once it reopens, you will be **logged in and authenticated**.
