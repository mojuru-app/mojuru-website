# General
Frequently Asked Questions

## Why isn't Mojuru on the App Store?
Various features inside the app are **not allowed or discouraged** by App Store guidelines.  
Publishing Mojuru there could result in the app being **removed**.

## Is Mojuru available for Android?
Not yet, but an Android release is **not excluded** in the future.
