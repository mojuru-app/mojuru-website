# Mojuru Plus

**Mojuru Plus** is the special edition of Mojuru, designed as a **thank you to our supporters**.  

> For the step-by-step guide on how to activate Mojuru Plus after your tip, see [Mojuru Plus Guide](/docs/guides/mojuru-plus).

By giving a tip, you can unlock Mojuru Plus and gain **exclusive perks** that enhance your experience in the app. Think of it as a way to **support the development** while enjoying extra features tailored for our tippers.

Tips are **crucial** to keep Mojuru active and maintained.

Your contribution ensures that Mojuru can continue to operate smoothly for all users.
